CSE 440 Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
(Fall 2025)

This course will focus on the fundamental issues underlying the design of intelligent systems. It covers classical AI topics including knowledge representation, reasoning, search, constraint satisfaction, application and current topics. The techniques students learn in the course will prepare you to solve real-world problems using intelligent systems. Course programming assignments will be in Python.
